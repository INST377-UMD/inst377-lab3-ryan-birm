How to play Rock Paper Scissors

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple game played between two people. Each player simultaneously chooses one of three options: rock, paper, or scissors. The winner is determined by the following rules:

  • Rock crushes scissors (rock wins)
  • Scissors cut paper (scissors win)
  • Paper covers rock (paper wins)
  • If both players choose the same option, it's a tie and they play again.
